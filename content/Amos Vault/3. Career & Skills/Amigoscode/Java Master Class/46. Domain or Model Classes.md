What are Domain Classes?
- Are classes that do not hold any business Logic.
- In Java they are <span style="color:rgb(255, 255, 0)">POJOs</span> .
- In Spring Boot they are <span style="color:rgb(255, 255, 0)">Beans</span> .
- They just reffer to the domain, Basically what data you are Storing.
- Eg Car Class, allows u to store a bunch of information about the car. Hence it is a Domain Class.
- The domain Classes just represents something in the Real World.
