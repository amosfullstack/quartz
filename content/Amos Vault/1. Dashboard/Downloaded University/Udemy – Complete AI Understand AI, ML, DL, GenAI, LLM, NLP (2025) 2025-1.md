## Description

Complete AI: Understand AI, ML, DL, GenAI, LLM, NLP (2025). This comprehensive course takes you from the basics of AI to generative AI. Whether you’re a beginner or looking to upgrade your skills, this course will set you on a clear path to success. This comprehensive course takes you from the basics of AI to advanced technologies like generative AI and natural language processing. With this course, you can strengthen your skills in AI and enter this hot and promising career field.

### What you will learn:

- AI Fundamentals: A deep understanding of AI, machine learning, and deep learning
- Supervised Learning: Mastering Supervised Learning Techniques and Their Real-World Applications
- Artificial Neural Networks: Building and training artificial neural networks and convolutional networks to solve complex problems
- Generative AI: Explore generative AI concepts such as GANs, transformers, and large language models.
- Natural Language Processing: Understanding how artificial intelligence understands and produces human language
- Hands-on experience with Python: Hands-on work with Python libraries such as Scikit-learn, TensorFlow, and PyTorch
- Developing AI applications: building chatbots, image classifiers, and text generators
- Differences between machine learning and deep learning: Understanding the differences and when to use each
- Practical Projects: Gain practical experience by working on AI projects

### Who is this course suitable for?

- Beginners: People who want to learn about artificial intelligence
- Intermediate level: People who want to improve their skills in artificial intelligence
- Everyone: This course is suitable for anyone interested in artificial intelligence.
