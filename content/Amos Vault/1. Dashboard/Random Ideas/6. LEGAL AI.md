#### AI LEGAL DOC ANALYZER

**1. AI-Powered Legal Document Analyzer for Tanzanian Laws**

**Problem:** Legal documents are complex. Many Tanzanian SMEs and citizens can't afford lawyers.

**Solution:** Build an AI system that:

- Parses Tanzanian laws and contracts
- Summarizes legal clauses in simple Swahili/English
- Detects risky clauses or conflicts with local laws
    

**Stack:**
- Laravel: User auth, document storage, API layer
- React: Frontend for document upload and summary viewer
- AI: GPT-4 Turbo + LangChain (with Tanzanian legal dataset)
- JS: PDF parsing, interactive clause highlighting
    
**Impact:** Useful for gov, law firms, and the public.
