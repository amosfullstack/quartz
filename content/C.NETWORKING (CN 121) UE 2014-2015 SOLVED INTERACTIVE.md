### **Question Two**

**a) What is Computer Networking; provide three arguments in favor of the use of a computer network in a modern organization and one argument against.** _(5 Marks)_

> [!Tip]- Answer (Click to show)  
> **Computer Networking is the interconnection of computers and devices to share resources and exchange information.**  
> **Arguments in Favor:**
> 
> 1. Resource sharing reduces costs
>     
> 2. Enhanced communication and collaboration
>     
> 3. Centralized data management and backup  
>     **Argument Against:**
>     
> 4. Security vulnerabilities and management complexity
>     



**b) Using their definitions, give the difference between LAN, MAN & WAN (include example of each).** _(6 Marks)_

> [!Tip]- Answer (Click to show)  
> **LAN:** Local area, single building/campus. _Example: Office network_  
> **MAN:** Metropolitan area, city-wide. _Example: City government network_  
> **WAN:** Wide area, countries/continents. _Example: Internet_



**c) What is the disadvantage associated with using a repeater? And what problem could occur as a result of too much traffic on a network?** _(5 Marks)_

> [!Tip]- Answer (Click to show)

> **Too much traffic problem: Network congestion and collisions**
